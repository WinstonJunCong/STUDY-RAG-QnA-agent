# Quick Setup Checklist

This is a condensed version of the full Getting Started guide. Use it as a launch-day checklist.

## ✅ Account & Workspace

- [ ] Sign up at app.novadesk.io/signup using your work email.
- [ ] Verify your email address.
- [ ] Choose your workspace subdomain (e.g., `acme.novadesk.io`).

## ✅ Team

- [ ] Invite agents via **Settings → Team → Invite Agents**.
- [ ] Assign roles: Agent, Team Lead, or Admin.

## ✅ Channels

- [ ] **Email** — Forward support@yourdomain.com to your NovaDesk inbox address.
- [ ] **Live Chat** — Paste the JS snippet into your website's `<head>` tag.
- [ ] **Social** — Connect Facebook and/or Twitter/X via OAuth.

## ✅ Automations

Set up at least one rule before you go live:
- Auto-reply acknowledging ticket receipt.
- Auto-assign tickets to the right team by keyword or tag.
- Escalation rule for tickets open longer than 24 hours.

## ✅ Knowledge Base

- [ ] Create your first Collection and Section.
- [ ] Publish at least one FAQ article.
- [ ] Confirm your help center loads at `yourcompany.novadesk.io/help`.

## ✅ Test Everything

Before announcing to customers:
1. Send a test email to your support address and confirm it creates a ticket.
2. Open your website and start a test chat session.
3. Search your knowledge base to confirm articles appear.
