# Getting Started with NovaDesk

Welcome! This guide walks you through setting up your NovaDesk account from scratch.

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.novadesk.io/signup](https://app.novadesk.io/signup).
2. Enter your work email and create a password.
3. Verify your email address using the link sent to your inbox.
4. Choose your subdomain (e.g., `yourcompany.novadesk.io`).

## Step 2 — Invite Your Team

Navigate to **Settings → Team → Invite Agents**.

- Enter the email addresses of your support agents.
- Assign each agent a role: **Agent**, **Team Lead**, or **Admin**.
- Agents will receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

## Step 3 — Connect Your Channels

Go to **Settings → Channels** to connect:

- **Email** — Forward your support email (e.g., support@yourcompany.com) to your NovaDesk inbox address, or configure SMTP/IMAP directly.
- **Live Chat** — Copy the embed snippet and paste it into your website's `<head>` tag.
- **Social** — Connect Facebook and Twitter/X via OAuth in the Social Channels section.

## Step 4 — Set Up Automations

Head to **Settings → Automations** to create your first rules.

Common starter automations:
- Auto-assign tickets tagged "billing" to the Billing team.
- Send an auto-reply acknowledging receipt within 5 minutes.
- Escalate tickets open for more than 24 hours to a Team Lead.

## Step 5 — Publish Your Knowledge Base

Go to **Knowledge Base → New Article**. Write your first help article and click **Publish**. Your knowledge base is accessible at `yourcompany.novadesk.io/help`.

## Need Help?

- In-app chat support is available 24/7 on Growth and Enterprise plans.
- Starter plan users can submit tickets at [support.novadesk.io](https://support.novadesk.io).
- Check our full documentation at [docs.novadesk.io](https://docs.novadesk.io).
