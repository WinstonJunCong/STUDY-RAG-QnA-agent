# Pricing & Plans — Frequently Asked Questions

## What plans does NovaDesk offer?

NovaDesk has three plans:

| Plan       | Price (per agent/month) | Agents Included |
|------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| Starter    | $19                    | Up to 3         |
| Growth     | $49                    | Up to 15        |
| Enterprise | Custom pricing         | Unlimited       |

All prices are billed monthly. Annual billing gives a **20% discount**.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new account gets a **14-day free trial** of the Growth plan with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you choose a plan or your account moves to read-only mode.

## What happens if I exceed my agent limit?

If you exceed the agent seats on your plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade. You can also purchase additional seats à la carte at the per-agent rate for your plan.

## Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Prorated credits are applied automatically.

## Does NovaDesk charge per ticket volume?

No. All plans include **unlimited tickets**. You are only limited by the number of agent seats.

## What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by bank transfer (ACH or wire).

## Are there any setup or onboarding fees?

There are no setup fees on Starter or Growth plans. Enterprise plans may include an optional **onboarding package** starting at $499, which covers dedicated setup assistance and staff training.

## Is there a non-profit or educational discount?

Yes. Non-profits and accredited educational institutions receive a **30% discount** on any plan. Contact sales@novadesk.io with proof of status to apply.
