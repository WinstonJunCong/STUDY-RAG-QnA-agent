# Plans & Billing — Common Questions

> **Note:** This article covers the same billing topics as the main Pricing FAQ but is written for customers who have already signed up.

## What are the available subscription plans?

NovaDesk currently offers three tiers:

- **Starter** — $19 per agent per month, supports up to 3 agents.
- **Growth** — $49 per agent per month, supports up to 15 agents.
- **Enterprise** — Custom pricing for teams needing unlimited agents and advanced security features.

Annual plans are available at a **20% discount** off the monthly rate.

## How does the 14-day free trial work?

New workspaces automatically start on a free 14-day trial of the Growth plan. No credit card is needed to start. When the trial ends, you can pick a paid plan or your workspace enters **read-only mode** (you can view data but not respond to tickets).

## Can I add more agents than my plan allows?

Yes. You can purchase additional agent seats at your plan's per-agent rate. Alternatively, you can upgrade to the next plan. If you're on Growth and need more than 15 agents, contact sales about an Enterprise plan.

## When do plan changes take effect?

- **Upgrades** — Immediate, with a prorated charge for the remaining cycle.
- **Downgrades** — Effective at the start of the next billing cycle; no immediate change.

## Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes — non-profits and educational institutions get a **30% discount**. Reach out to sales@novadesk.io with proof of your status.

## Which payment methods does NovaDesk accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can arrange bank transfer (ACH or SWIFT wire).

## Where can I find my invoices?

Invoices are in **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. They are also emailed to the billing contact after each charge.
