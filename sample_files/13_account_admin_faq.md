# Account & Admin Settings — FAQ

## How do I change my workspace name or subdomain?

Go to **Settings → General → Workspace**. You can update the display name anytime. Changing the subdomain is only allowed **once** and will break any bookmarked or embedded links using the old URL. Contact support before changing your subdomain.

## What are the agent roles?

| Role       | Permissions                                                         |
|------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Agent      | View and respond to tickets assigned to them or their team          |
| Team Lead  | All Agent permissions + manage team members, view team reports      |
| Admin      | Full access — manage all settings, billing, integrations, and data  |

## How do I deactivate an agent?

Go to **Settings → Team**, find the agent, and click **Deactivate**. Deactivated agents can no longer log in, but their ticket history is preserved. Reactivate at any time.

## Can I transfer account ownership?

Yes. The current owner can go to **Settings → General → Transfer Ownership**, select another Admin, and confirm. The previous owner retains Admin access but loses owner-only privileges (like deleting the workspace).

## How do I delete my workspace?

Only the workspace owner can delete the account. Go to **Settings → General → Danger Zone → Delete Workspace**. You'll be required to type your workspace name to confirm. **This action is irreversible.** All data is permanently deleted after a 30-day grace period, during which you can cancel the deletion.

## Can I have multiple workspaces?

Yes. You can create separate workspaces for different brands or products under one login. Each workspace is billed independently.

## How do I configure business hours?

Go to **Settings → Business Hours** and set your operating days and times. Business hours affect SLA calculations and the live chat widget's offline mode display.

## Can I restrict agents to specific teams or inboxes?

Yes. Create **Teams** under Settings → Teams and assign agents to them. You can then configure ticket routing rules and inbox permissions per team, so agents only see relevant tickets.
